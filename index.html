Containers

.container class is a responsive, fixed-width container, meaning its max-width changes at each breakpoint.

Use .container-fluid for a full width container, spanning the entire width of the viewport.

.container-sm is 100% wide to start until the small breakpoint is reached.
.container-md is 100% wide until medium breakpoint is reached.
.container-lg is 100% wide until large breakpoint is reached.
.container-xl is 100% wide until extra large breakpoint is reached.
